## TOKENS NEEDED

| Token | Quantity | Use |
|-------|----------|-----|
| Runner tokens | 4 | Mark base runners |
| Ball tokens | 4 | Track ball count |
| Strike tokens | 3 | Track strike count |
| Out tokens | 3 | Track outs |
| Seeing It token | 1 | Batter bonus marker |

Use coins, chips, or small objects.

---

## SELECTION CARDS

Each player needs cards or tokens to select secretly:

**PITCHER (9 cards or dial):**
- 3 pitch types: FB, CH, CB
- Strike locations: UP, DOWN, IN, OUT, MID
- Ball locations: HIGH, LOW, INSIDE, OUTSIDE

**BATTER (8 cards or dial):**
- Actions: SWING, TAKE
- Pitch guesses: FB, CH, CB, NONE
- Location guesses: UP, DOWN, IN, OUT, MID, NONE

---

## SETUP

1. Place play mat between players
2. Each player gets their cards (Pitcher + Batter)
3. Decide home/visitor (coin flip)
4. Visitor bats first
5. Play ball!
