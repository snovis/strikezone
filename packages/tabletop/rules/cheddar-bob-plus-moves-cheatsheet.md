## PLUS MOVES

### POWER+ (Batter declares before battle)
*"Swinging for the fences"*

| Battle Result | Effect |
|---------------|--------|
| **WIN** | Tier +1 (weak‚Üísolid‚Üístrong). Already strong? +1 to result roll |
| **LOSE** | Tier -1 (strong‚Üísolid‚Üíweak). Already weak? **Swinging K** |

### CONTACT+ (Batter declares before battle)
*"Just get on base"*

| Battle Result | Effect |
|---------------|--------|
| **WIN** | Result ‚â§4 = **WALK** (instead of out). Max hit = single |
| **LOSE** | Runners do NOT advance on the out |

### FASTBALL+ or CURVEBALL+ (Pitcher declares before battle)
*"Going for the strikeout"*

| Battle Result | Effect |
|---------------|--------|
| **WIN** | +2 to result roll (toward K zone) |
| **LOSE** | Batter gets **FREE BASE** (walk) |

## EXAMPLE AT-BAT

```
Pitcher: FASTBALL+ (going for K)
Batter:  POWER+ (swinging for fences)

MATCHUP: FB vs Power = Batter +1

BATTLE:
  Pitcher rolls [4,3] = 7
  Batter rolls [5,4] +1 = 10
  ‚Üí BATTER WINS (solid tier)
  ‚Üí POWER+ bonus: tier becomes STRONG

RESULT (batter rolls):
  [3,5] = 8, Strong tier
  ‚Üí 2B (double!)

If pitcher had won, POWER+ penalty would drop batter's
tier AND pitcher's FASTBALL+ would add +2 to result...
likely a strikeout!
```
