# THE GAME

You are **playing baseball decisions**. Each at-bat is a battle of wits and dice between pitcher and batter. Choose your approach, commit to your selection, and let the dice decide.

**Core Philosophy:**
- Weak execution gives the opponent opportunities
- Strong execution limits opportunities
- The player trying to accomplish something rolls
- Snake eyes = critical failure for whoever rolled

---

# TURN FLOW

```
1. SELECT    Both secretly pick Approach + Location/Setup
2. STEAL?    Runner announces "going!" (commits blind)
3. REVEAL    Show choices simultaneously
   ‚Üí If steal: Resolve steal roll, then back to step 1
4. BATTLE    Both roll 2d6 + modifiers, higher wins (ties reroll)
5. RESULT    Winner rolls for outcome
6. EXTRAS    Runner advancement, stretch attempts, etc.
```

**High-stakes timing:** After SELECT, before REVEAL. Runner commits blind!

# PHASE 5: RESULT

The **winner** rolls 2d6 on their result table.

**No modifiers on result roll** - tier already determines your column.

## Batter Wins ‚Üí Batter Rolls 2d6

| Roll |  Weak   | Solid | Strong |
| ---- | :-----: | :---: | :----: |
| ‚â§7   | **OUT** | OUT   |   1B   |
| 8-9  |   OUT   |  1B   |  2B+   |
| 10+  |   1B    |  2B+  | **HR** |

- **Snake eyes = OUT** (critical failure)
- **+ = Stretch opportunity** (battle roll)

## Pitcher Wins ‚Üí Pitcher Rolls 2d6

| Roll | Weak | Solid | Strong |
|------|:----:|:-----:|:------:|
| ‚â§7 | **BB** | O+RA | K+RC |
| 8-9 | O+RA | K+RC | O-RF |
| 10+ | O+RC | O-RF | **DP!** |

**Legend:**
- **BB** = Walk (batter reaches base)
- **O** = Out (batter is out)
- **K** = Strikeout (batter is out)
- **RA** = Runners Advance freely
- **RC** = Runners advance with Challenge (battle roll, tie ‚Üí runner)
- **RF** = Runners Frozen (no advancement)
- **DP!** = Guaranteed Double Play (if runner on 1st)

- **Snake eyes = HBP** (batter reaches, critical failure for pitcher)

# BASERUNNING

## Battle Rolls (Steal & Stretch)

**Tie goes to the runner!**

### Stretch Attempt (Batter rolled 1B+ or 2B+)

**Battle Roll:** Both roll 2d6, no modifiers.

| Result | Outcome |
|--------|---------|
| Batter higher or tie | **SAFE** at extra base |
| Pitcher higher | **OUT** (caught stretching) |
| üêç Batter | **OUT** - Critical failure |
| üé≤ Batter rolls 12 | **SAFE** - Auto success |
| üêç Pitcher | **SAFE** - Critical failure |
| üé≤ Pitcher rolls 12 | **OUT** - Auto success |

### Runner Advancement on Outs

When pitcher gets an out with runners on base:

**Ground Ball - Double Play Attempt**
If runner on 1st, **Pitcher rolls 2d6:**

| Roll | Result |
|------|--------|
| 2 (snake eyes) | Error - all runners safe, batter safe |
| 3-8 | Single out only, runners advance |
| 9-10 | Single out, runners hold |
| 11+ | **DOUBLE PLAY** |

**Fly Ball / Line Out - Tag Up Attempt**
If runner on 3rd, **Batter rolls 2d6:**

| Roll | Result |
|------|--------|
| 2 (snake eyes) | Runner thrown out at home |
| 3-6 | Runner holds |
| 7+ | Runner scores (sac fly) |

*Modify based on out type: Lineout = harder, Deep fly = easier*

### Runner Advancement on Hits

| Hit Type | Tier | Runner on 1st | Runner on 2nd |
|----------|------|---------------|---------------|
| Single | Any | To 2nd | To 3rd |
| Double | Weak | To 3rd | Scores |
| Double | Solid | To 3rd | Scores |
| Double | Strong | **Scores** | Scores |
| Triple | Any | Scores | Scores |
| HR | Any | Scores | Scores |

# PLUS MOVES

Declare plus moves during SELECT phase (before reveal).

## POWER+ (Batter)
*"Swinging for the fences"*

| You... | Effect |
|--------|--------|
| **WIN battle** | Tier +1 (weak‚Üísolid, solid‚Üístrong). Already strong? +1 to result |
| **LOSE battle** | Tier -1 (strong‚Üísolid, solid‚Üíweak). Already weak? **Swinging K** |

## CONTACT+ (Batter)
*"Just get on base"*

| You... | Effect |
|--------|--------|
| **WIN battle** | Result ‚â§4 = **WALK**. Max hit = single (no extra bases) |
| **LOSE battle** | Runners do NOT advance on the out |

## FASTBALL+ or CURVEBALL+ (Pitcher)
*"Going for the strikeout"*

| You... | Effect |
|--------|--------|
| **WIN battle** | +2 to result roll (pushes toward K zone) |
| **LOSE battle** | Batter gets **FREE BASE** (walk) |

*Changeup has no plus move - it's the safe middle option.*

# DESIGN NOTES

**Why this works:**
1. **Situation management** - You're playing baseball decisions, not simulating pitches
2. **Risk/reward** - Every plus move is a bet
3. **Agency** - "Who wants it, rolls it" gives control to the acting player
4. **Tension** - Simultaneous selection, snake eyes drama
5. **Tactile** - Fidget selectors, dice rolling satisfaction

**Future: Player Cards**
- Power hitters: +1 to strong tier results
- Speedsters: +2 to steal/stretch rolls
- Contact specialists: +1 to getting on base
- Pitchers: Bonus to specific pitch types
